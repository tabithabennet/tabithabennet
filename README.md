## 🚀 Featured Projects

> Real-world products solving real problems — spanning health, sport, science & space.

### 🧬 [MedTrack Dashboard](#)
**Problem:** Patients managing chronic conditions have no simple way to visualise their health data over time and share meaningful summaries with their doctors.  
**Solution:** A clean, patient-facing health data dashboard that lets users log symptoms, medication, mood, and vitals — then generates shareable summaries for clinical appointments.  
**Stack:** `React` `Python` `Chart.js` `Figma`  
> 🏥 *Intersection of MedTech + Product Design*

---

### 🐴 [PoloIQ — Handicap & Performance Tracker](#)
**Problem:** Polo clubs manage player handicaps, tournament entries, and horse rotation through spreadsheets and WhatsApp groups.  
**Solution:** A web app for polo clubs to manage player profiles, horse assignments, handicap tracking, and tournament scheduling in one place.  
**Stack:** `React` `JavaScript` `Figma` `Airtable API`  
> 🏆 *Built from direct experience at The Hamptons Polo Club*

---

### 🌍 [Daily Habit Tracker](https://euphonious-genie-c0896d.netlify.app)
**Problem:** Goal-tracking apps are either too complex or don't show behavioural patterns meaningfully.  
**Solution:** A single-page React app with streak tracking, progress bars, and behavioural analytics. Briefly launched as a company.  
**Stack:** `React` `React Router` `localStorage` `CSS Animations`  
> 🚀 *[Live Demo ↗](https://euphonious-genie-c0896d.netlify.app)*

---

### 🧬 [GeneExplorer](#)
**Problem:** Genetic data is complex, inaccessible, and rarely visualised in a way non-scientists can explore.  
**Solution:** An interactive platform to browse gene profiles, explore genetic traits, and visualise relationships between genetics through intuitive, data-driven UI.  
**Stack:** `JavaScript` `D3.js` `SPA Architecture` `Figma`  
> 🔬 *Intersection of BioTech + Data Visualisation*

---

### 🔭 [SpaceNow — Live Space Events Feed](#)
**Problem:** Space enthusiasts have no single, beautiful place to track upcoming launches, ISS passes, and astronomical events.  
**Solution:** A live-updating dashboard pulling from NASA & SpaceX APIs displaying upcoming events, countdowns, and ISS position on a globe.  
**Stack:** `React` `NASA API` `SpaceX API` `Globe.gl`  
> 🌌 *For the space-obsessed*

---

### 💊 [VITL Holistic](#)
**Problem:** The wellness industry lacks brands that credibly bridge science-backed health information with holistic lifestyle design.  
**Solution:** Building a wellness brand with a digital-first presence — visual identity, content strategy, and a product roadmap grounded in behavioural science.  
**Stack:** `Webflow` `Figma` `Brand Strategy`  
> ✨ *Currently in development*
